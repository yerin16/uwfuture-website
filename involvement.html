Become a Member

Members may engage with the U&W organization either directly or through affiliation with the U&W Youth Club. Individuals not enrolled in schools hosting a U&W Youth Club can seek membership by completing the application form available on the organization's official website. Membership spans a single academic year and automatically renews with the commencement of each subsequent academic year. All members are expected to fulfill their obligations to be eligible for end-of-year benefits.

Benefits

  • Receive U&W certificate
  • Publish personal work on WCEN and U&W annual magazine

    • Duties

  • Youth Reporter (Choose 1*)
    • 2 Reports
    • 1 Report + 1 Speech (Able to apply for U&W Ambassador*)
  • Any form of donation

    Start Your U&W Youth Club

    School-enrolled individuals interested in experiencing leadership through U&W activities can establish a U&W Youth Club at their school. U&W offers guidance and ongoing support to facilitate club operations throughout the year. As with all members, U&W Youth Club leaders are expected to fulfill their membership duties.

    Benefits

  • Receive U&W certificate
  • Become U&W Ambassador for first academic year
    and able to attend U&W Future Forum
  • Publish personal work on WCEN and U&W annual magazine

    • Duties

  • Lead U&W Youth Club for at least one academic year
  • Youth Reporter (Same as above*)
  • Hold fundrasider at school and make donations
