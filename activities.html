Academic Fields

U&W Activities engage in a diverse range of activities across five academic fields: Health & Human Services, Technology & Innovation, Humanities & Culture, Music & Arts, and Sustainability & Environmental Management. Through hands-on projects, collaborative research, and creative endeavors, they explore and contribute to these dynamic areas, preparing for impactful careers and meaningful contributions to society.

Main Activities

U&W offers three main activities: U&W Ambassador, Future, and Youth Activities. These programs provide students with opportunities to develop leadership skills, engage in forward-thinking projects, and participate in enriching experiences across our five academic fields. Members are involved in cultural exchanges and share opinions on a wide range of topics, fostering a dynamic and inclusive community.

01

U&W Ambassador

  • Have the opportunity to attend the annual virtual forum and receive ambassador certificates for following academic year.
  • Receive funding for personal research and cultural exchange trips.
02

U&W Future Forum

  • Hold annual virtual forum featuring ambassadors from various countries, where they deliver speeches about personal and group project.
  • Publish work of all memebers in the annual magazine.

Activity Timeline

U&W offers three main activities: U&W Ambassador, Future, and Youth Activities. These programs provide students with opportunities to develop leadership skills, engage in forward-thinking projects, and participate in enriching experiences across our five academic fields. Members are involved in cultural exchanges and share opinions on a wide range of topics, fostering a dynamic and inclusive community.

  • Aug-Sept
    Member Recruitment

    Students may submit their applications through the official U&W website or enroll in the U&W Youth Club if offered at their school.

  • Oct-Dec
    1st Semester

    Members will engage in personal projects, such as delivering speeches or composing reports, and will also organize donation drives.

  • Jan-Mar
    2nd Semester

    Members will submit personal work and collaborate on a group project. U&W Ambassadors will be announced in February.

  • April
    U&W Future Forum

    U&W Ambassadors from various schools will showcase group project and deliver speeches at the forum, with all works published in the magazine.
