What We Do

U&W Ambassador

U&W Ambassadors are selected through application each year, have opportunity to attend annual U&W forum for selected year, and receive support from U&W for cultural exchange program or personal research.

U&W Future Forum

U&W holds global forum that encompasses various academic fields from techhnology to arts. U&W Ambassadors from different countries have opportunity to present U&W Future project and are able to be part of cultural exchange.

U&W Youth Activities

U&W Youth are part of youth reporter program and publish speech and reports annually. Youths are able to establish U&W Youth Club at their own school to recruit peers whose quality aligns with U&W misson and core values.

Our locations

U&W is based in Seoul, South Korea and we have U&W Youth Clubs in 3 different countries —United States, South Korea, and Thailand.
